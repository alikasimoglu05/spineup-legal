Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SpineUp ("SpineUp", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the SpineUp mobile application.

Information We Collect

SpineUp is designed to analyze posture using photos that you choose to take or upload. Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be used:

  • Photos you take with the camera or select from your photo library for posture analysis.
  • Posture analysis results, reports, selected preferences, language settings, and profile details that you enter in the app.
  • Subscription and purchase status provided by Apple and RevenueCat, such as product identifiers, entitlement status, and receipt-related information.
  • Technical information needed for app functionality, diagnostics, purchase restoration, and subscription management.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Generate posture analysis and reports.
  • Save your reports, preferences, and app settings on your device.
  • Provide premium subscription access, restore purchases, and manage subscription status.
  • Improve reliability, security, and the overall app experience.
  • Respond to support requests that you send to us.

Photos and Posture Data

Photos used for posture analysis are processed by the app for the purpose of creating posture reports. SpineUp does not use your photos for advertising and does not sell your photos or posture data.

Reports and related app data may be stored locally on your device so you can view your history and compare progress. If you delete the app or reset app data, locally stored information may be removed from your device.

Subscriptions and Payments

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. SpineUp uses RevenueCat to help validate purchases, manage subscription entitlements, restore purchases, and present subscription management tools. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Apple's privacy practices are described at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/. RevenueCat's privacy practices are described at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/.

Third-Party Services

SpineUp may use third-party services that support app functionality, including Apple App Store services and RevenueCat. These services may process limited technical, purchase, or device-related information according to their own privacy policies.

Tracking and Advertising

SpineUp does not sell personal information. SpineUp does not use your photos or posture reports for third-party advertising. SpineUp does not use third-party advertising tracking in the app.

Health and Wellness Notice

SpineUp provides posture-related information for awareness and tracking purposes only. The app is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice. If you have pain, discomfort, or a medical concern, consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Data Retention

Information stored locally in the app remains on your device unless you delete it, reset the app, or remove the app. Subscription records may be retained by Apple and RevenueCat as needed for purchase validation, account management, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and support.

Children's Privacy

SpineUp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You can choose not to grant camera or photo library access. Some posture analysis features may not work without photo access. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple Account settings. You can delete locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

International Use

If you use SpineUp outside your country of residence, information may be processed in countries where Apple, RevenueCat, or their service providers operate. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your region.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and make the revised policy available through the app or the public policy URL.
